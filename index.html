About Us

IRCA is the industry leader of integrated software product development, IT services and digital marketing.

We are rapidly growing Software Product Development Company and are committed to create long-term values for our clients by identifying both risks and opportunities in the marketplace. We are an IT consulting, software development & services and Digital Marketing service providers headquartered in Bangalore, India with operations in Middle East and other places. Technology, vision and execution are the touchstones of our philosophy and what we stand by in our most challenging endeavors. This, coupled with our strong financial backing, allows us to deliver best in the class industry products.

We have 700+ happy clients across global over past decades. Our key strengths are our people, operational efficiency, quality engineering, core values, design-driven innovation, technology expertise, and agile mind-set. We are focused and innovative to provide the best software product development outputs, web design, web development, mobile application development, IT consulting services, marketing services, custom products and cloud operations. Our array of products span different industry verticals such as hospitality management, club management, inventory management, sales force automation, human resource management and risk analysis and mitigation.
