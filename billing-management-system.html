"BMS" - Billing management system

One retail POS Billing for all retail businesses "IRCA's Billing Management software"

Home / BMS

BMS simplifies the retail billing, customer transactions and manages the stock effectively. It is ready billing Software solves all your issues of managing the outlet.

Our billing software used to keep track of stock items, no more worries of handing the stock, sales bill generation, Invoice and other back-end process of generation of reports. System can be used to manage the stock which helps to find shortage of stock, fast-moving items can be pre-ordered before stock closed down.

IRCA's Billing Management System includes

  • Retail Outlet automation
  • Automate your business hassle free
  • Manage Companies and multiple outlets
  • Access restriction based on Permission for each stakeholders
  • Monitor real-time sales of retail outlet
  • Auto backup facility helps easy recovery of retail store data
  • Set up low inventory stock alerts
  • GST Ready Invoice template with all required GST features
  • Easily manage ageing stock items
  • Easily manage purchase return and sales return
